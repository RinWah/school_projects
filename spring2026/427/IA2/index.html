Maria

First-generation student & part-time barista

Maria is a first generation student and part-time barista. She is balancing school alongside her part-time job as a barista while managing her own bills and her family's bills. She's frustrated with inaccurate bank statements and unexpected fees, but she is determined to build strong goals through weekly progress.

Goals

  • Build an emergency fund with $1,000 in it.
  • Save $600 for her spring semesteer textbooks.
  • Save $1,200 within the next year for her first month's rent when she moves out from her family's home.

Frustrations

  • Hidden fees: "I hate hidden fees like shipping or processing."
  • Inaccurate bank statements: "My card gets declined even though I have money. Pending vs. available is the worst."
  • Difficulty reading bank statements: "When the merchant name is unrecognizable or there's a surprise hold, I shouldn't have to call customer service."
